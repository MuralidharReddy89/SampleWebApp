The Indian Premier League (IPL) (also known as the TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons) is a men's Twenty20 (T20) cricket league that is annually held in India.

After playing a series of matches, four teams will be qualified for the semi-finals. In the end, only one team will win the IPL claiming the trophy and set prize money. The prize money received will be equally distributed among all the players of the team. The winning team will get 200 million INR for winning the IPL..
